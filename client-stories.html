SAP Co-Innovation Lab
Data & Product
10 years of project data sitting unused. I turned it into visual insights, found a product gap in the market, and got a new product proposal approved by the executive board.
10 yearsdata analyzedBoard approvednew productPortfoliolaunched
The Challenge
SAP's Co-Innovation Lab in Palo Alto had accumulated a decade of project data across pipelines, partnerships, and innovation experiments — but nobody had ever made sense of it all. The data sat in different systems, in different formats, telling no story. Leadership knew there were insights buried in there but had no capacity to extract them. They also needed to justify the lab's impact and find the next big opportunity.
How I Solved It
I extracted project data from SAP pipelines and integrated it with project fishtank data, cleaning and structuring a decade's worth of information. I then designed an infographic that visualized 10 years of co-innovation achievements — which became a key communication tool for the lab. I also created podcasts and animated videos to tell the value proposition story through effective storytelling. Meanwhile, I initiated independent market research that revealed a clear gap for a new product. I wrote the proposal and presented it to the executive board, who approved it. I then developed go-to-market plans and launched a portfolio of networking projects that are now integral to SAP Cloud Operations.

Doktor.se — Digital Health
Growth
A digital health startup with limited budget needed rapid growth. I generated over 1M Kr. in revenue through data-driven marketing with 2x ROI on every campaign.
1M+ Kr.revenue2x ROIcampaigns1,000+customers
The Challenge
Doktor.se was a digital healthcare platform in Sweden that needed to grow fast — more website traffic, more patients signing up, more revenue. But the marketing budget was tight, so every krona spent had to deliver measurable returns. Previous campaigns had been scattered with no clear strategy or performance tracking.
How I Solved It
I started by auditing all existing marketing channels to find where the highest ROI opportunities were hiding. Then I ran systematic A/B tests across SEO, social media, and paid channels to find what actually worked. Once I identified the winners — paid search, display advertising, and retargeting — I scaled those aggressively while cutting underperformers. I also built an email marketing and lead generation engine to acquire and retain customers over time, creating a sustainable growth loop rather than one-off campaigns.
